Paint The Future Black or PTFB never limit themselves with any musical style, but are mostly classified as alternative rock. The band started their musical career in September 2015 in Minsk, Belarus. Following two singles “Scars On The Face”(2015) and “Stairway Without Steps”(2016) Paint The Future Black released their debut EP-album “Anthracite”(2017) including 6 songs that can be downloaded in most popular online stores. This recording is about our future, which is as black as the darkest coal, but all of us able to kindle the brightest flame with this coal.

Current line-up:

Phil Cribs - clean vocals

Kery Jinxed - extreme vocals

Misha Wide - guitar

Harry Jordison - drums

Alex RA - bass guitar

Recorded by Dmitry Mikulich and Arseny Nikonov at various studios in Minsk, Belarus
Mixed and mastered by Roman Kharyukov
Artwork by Alena Hodor
